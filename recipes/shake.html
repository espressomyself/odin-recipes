Ultra Chocolate Protein Shake

This ultra chocolate protein shake combines the best of both worlds for gym-goers with a sweet tooth.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup of milk
  • 1 banana
  • Handful of rolled oats
  • 1 serving of protein powder
  • 1 tablespoon of cocoa powder
  • Vanilla sweetener or sugar

Steps

  1. Pour milk into a blender
  2. Add banana (fresh or frozen)
  3. Add protein powder, cocoa powder, and desired sweetener
  4. Blend for 30 seconds
  5. Add oats
  6. Blend for another 30 seconds to 1 minute
  7. Pour the liquid gold into a cup and enjoy
