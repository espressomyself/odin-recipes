Chicken Quinoa Protein Bowl

Being healthy doesn't mean giving up on taste. Enjoy a low-calorie, heart healthy meal of mediterranean-styled chicken quinoa protein bowl.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups of quinoa
  • 1 pound of chicken breast
  • 2 gloves garlic
  • 2 tablespoons of honey
  • 1 container of hummus
  • 1 tomato
  • 1 cucumber
  • 2 bell peppers
  • 3/4 cup lemon juice

Instructions

  1. Prepare hot skillet and add garlic to sautee
  2. Turn on a second eye for quinoa and start to boil
  3. Cut chicken into bite-sized pieces and add to skillet once garlic gives aroma
  4. Once chicken is halfway done cooking, add honey to the skillet and spread onto chicken
  5. Prepare veggies by chopping into small pieces and set to the side
  6. Prepare hummus dressing by mixing half the container of hummus with lemon juice
  7. Once chicken and quinoa are finished cooking, veggies and dressing can be added
  8. Enjoy cold or hot!
