Berry Yogurt Parfait

This berry yogurt parfait is a perfect energy-booster to kickstart your day

Ingredients

  • Greek Yogurt
  • Granola
  • Nuts (i.e. walnut, pecan, cashew)
  • Berries (i.e. strawberries, blueberries, raspberries)
  • Coconut Flakes
  • Honey

Instructions

  1. Make first layer with several spoonfuls of yogurt
  2. Atop the yogurt base with granola, berries, and nuts
  3. Add another layer of yogurt
  4. Finish with all toppings (granola, nuts, berries, coconut flakes, and honey)
